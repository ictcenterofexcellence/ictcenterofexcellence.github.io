Whitepapers ​

As a result of our investigations, the center of excellence has written several whitepapers with our investigation results. You can find these papers below:

Model-based testing ​

  • Model-Based Testing: Improved Quality-Assurance with less effort

Model-Based Testing (MBT) is a novel testing approach that automates exploratory testing. By automating, this process can be done faster, more systematically, and fully automated, for example during downtime at night.

Digital twins ​

  • The evolution and future of Digital Twin Technology

Let’s explore the evolution and future of Digital Twins; how it started, how they’ve evolved, the different types of Digital Twins, the profound impact they’ve had on industries, and what the future holds for this innovative technology.

Model-Driven engineering ​

  • Transforming how we develop reliable and robust software with Model-Driven Engineering

We believe Model-Driven Engineering can be valuable to create high quality, maintainable software, even for extremely complex and evolving systems. In this whitepaper our High Tech Center of Excellence will explain why and how.

Computer vision ​

  • Computer Vision: cost-effective model training with Digital Twin synthetic data

Computer vision problems can be solved using traditional vision algorithms or machine learning (ML) vision models. This paper presents a solution to getting the training images by virtually generating synthetic training images in the game engine Unity3D. This has the potential to greatly speed up training and iteration time, as gathering of input images becomes much simpler.
